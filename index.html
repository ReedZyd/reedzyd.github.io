 Research Experiences

Active object tracking (AOT) based on spatial-temporal attention


  • Research keywords: active object tracking, spatial-temporal attention, multi-agent reinforcement learning (MARL)
  • A novel spatial-temporal attention representation is proposed to encode the target location, scene structure and temporal cues dynamically.
  • Video tracker, spatial-temporal attention representation module and policy learning are integrated into an adversarial MARL pipeline for a generalizable AOT tracker.
  • The framework generalizes well in photo-realistic and real-world scenes (video clips and robots, e.g. Scout, Scout-mini), and significantly outperforms the SOTA methods.
  • Re-implementation of AOT methods based on RL, algorithm design, experiments on real robots (Scout, Scout-mini).

    Design and implementation of a face recognition and age/gender estimation system


  • Excellent graduation thesis in Shandong University, 2019.
  • Design and realize a real-time face recognition and age/gender estimation system on a 43-inch vertical all-in-one.
  • Based on multi-thread technology, face recognition is carried out by Seeta Face, and age estimation is based on SSR-Net.
  • The human-computer interface is written based on Qt.
  • Algorithm re-implementation, human-computer interface design and realization.
  • Overall pipeline design, age estimation network training, human-computer interface design and realization.
